Fettuccine Alfredo

Fettuccine alfredo is a classic Italian pasta dish that features a rich and creamy sauce made with butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 pint heavy cream

Instructions

  • Cook the pasta in salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  • In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  • Gradually whisk in the Parmesan cheese until it has melted and the sauce is smooth. If the sauce is too thick, add pasta water a tablespoon at a time until it reaches the desired consistency.
  • Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat. If the sauce seems too thick, add more pasta water as needed.
  • Garnish with chopped parsley, if desired, and serve immediately.
  • Enjoy your homemade fettuccine alfredo!
